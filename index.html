石大易班发展中心

“石大易班”是中国石油大学（华东）依托教育部“易班网”建设而成的学生综合互动社区。

易班网是高校教育教学，生活服务，文化娱乐的大学生综合网络互动社区平台，为在校师生提供主页定制，在线学习，活动发布，兴趣交友等教育信息化一站式服务功能。

Do you know about our university?

China University of Petroleum (UPC) is a national key university direct and “985 ly affiliated to the Ministry of Education and a member of the “211 Project”innovation platform for preponderant discipline” universities. China University of Petroleum is a higher education institution co-constructed by the Ministry of Education, four leading petroleum and petrochemical companies (CNPC, SINOPEC, CNOOC, and CHEMCHINA) and the People’s Government of Shandong Province. Honored as “the cradle of scientific and technological talents for petroleum industry”, UPC is an important base of training high-level talents for petroleum and petrochemical industry and has already developed into a multi-disciplinary, well-rounded university focusing on petroleum and engineering.
